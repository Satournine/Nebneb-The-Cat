This is Nebneb

Nebneb, a charming tabby, entered the world in 2018, bringing joy to her owners' lives with her playful demeanor and endearing purrs. Nebneb's distinctive markings and captivating green eyes make her a unique and cherished feline companion. Despite Nebneb's captivating charm, she possesses a gentle and reserved nature, making her incredibly shy. While her unique tabby fur and adorable features draw admiration, she often prefers the comfort of solitude, capturing the hearts of those who understand and respect her need for quiet moments.
